# Business-Analytics-Research
This assignment focuses on building an understanding of key concepts and terminology used in Business Analytics. It requires researching and defining 38 important terms related to analytics foundations, data and reporting, customer and sales KPIs, and marketing analytics. The purpose of the assignment is to develop a strong vocabulary in business analytics, distinguish between related concepts, and show how these terms are applied in real business situations through practical examples.

The assignment also emphasizes the importance of using credible sources, applying proper Harvard referencing, and demonstrating an understanding of how businesses use data to support decision-making and measure performance. Through this research, students gain foundational knowledge in analytics concepts such as predictive analysis, KPIs, customer metrics, and campaign performance, which are essential for further study and practical application in the field of business analytics.
